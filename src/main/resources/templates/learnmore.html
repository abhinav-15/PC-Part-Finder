Pc Part Finder

PcPartFinder is a web-based application made to help you in locating shops that can repair your device be it with Hardware Issue or Software. Our site makes the process of finding trust worthy shops easier just by giving some basic input like city name, the device you use and the issues device faces. Our project aims to streamline the search for repair services and enhance the user experience by providing targeted results based on individual requirements.

How to Use PcPartFinder: Step-by-Step Tutorial

  1. Sign Up or Log In: Create an account or log in to access the platform.
  2. Find all shops in your City : Specify city and find all verified the shops in your city.
  3. Find shops for your device: Specify city, Device Name, Hardware issue and / or Software issue and find the shops around you that provide that service.
  4. Results Display: View a list of repair shops based on your criteria and choose the one that seems best to you.
  5. Shop Details: Learn more about a shop by clicking on its view details.
  6. Get Directions: Click on Get Directions button below any shop to move directly to Google Maps and find exact route to the shop.
  7. Contact Shop: Connect with a repair shop using provided contact information.
  8. Log Out: Sign out when done.
