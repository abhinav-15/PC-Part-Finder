Pc Part Finder

PcPartFinder is a web-based application made to help you in locating shops that can repair your device be it with Hardware Issue or Software. Our site makes the process of finding trust worthy shops easier just by giving some basic input like city name, the device you use and the issues device faces. Our project aims to streamline the search for repair services and enhance the user experience by providing targeted results based on individual requirements.

How to Use PcPartFinder: Step-by-Step Tutorial

  1. Homepage Introduction: Learn how PcPartFinder can help you find the right repair shops.
  2. Sign Up or Log In: Create an account or log in to access the platform.
  3. Search for Repair Shops: Specify city, device type, and issues to find matches.
  4. Results Display: View a list of repair shops based on your criteria.
  5. Shop Details: Learn more about a shop by clicking on its listing.
  6. Contact Shop: Connect with a repair shop using provided contact information.
  7. Log Out: Sign out when done.
